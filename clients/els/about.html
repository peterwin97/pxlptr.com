Hi, I'm Emily.

I've always had a deep passion for being creative and helping others find their voice.

I studied marketing at Newman University and Wichita State University — not because I wanted to learn how to sell things, but because I wanted to understand how people connect with what they love. That question has shaped everything I've done since.

In July 2023, I relocated to Dallas, Texas to advance my marketing expertise and be somewhere that was building itself. I wanted to help shape what it became. Since then, I've had the privilege of working with brands across every stage — from first-time founders with a great idea and a blank Instagram grid, to established businesses ready to grow into something bigger.

Every brand I work with gets the same thing: my full attention, my genuine curiosity about what they're making and why, and a commitment to translating that into something people can actually feel.

When I'm not working, I'm walking Ollie and Beau, taking fitness classes, cooking something ambitious with my boyfriend, or looking for the next place worth photographing.
